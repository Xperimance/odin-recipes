Mac and Cheese

Description

This easy-to-make mac and cheese is an instant winner for any occasion. Whether it's for family gatherings or impressing your guests, no one can say no to a nice plate of mac and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 package elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 2 eggs, well beaten
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 pinch paprika

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a boil. Stir in the macaroni. Cook the pasta until it's tender, yet firm. Drain the water and put the pasta in a slow cooker.
  2. Add butter to the pasta and stir until melted; season with salt and pepper. Sprinkle about 1/2 of the Cheddar cheese over the pasta and stir together.
  3. Whisk evaporated milk and eggs together in a bowl. Stir it into the pasta mixture.

Tacos

Description

Whether you're looking for the perfect party appetiser or something to feed your spice cravings, tacos are always the go-to meal for any occassion.

Here at Odin, we present to you the best taco recipe that you'll ever need to impress your guests and friends the next time you have to host a party.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  • 8 corn tortillas

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees.
  2. Grill the chicken. Keep cooking until the chicken is no longer pink at the center.
  3. Heat the corn tortillas until soft in the microwave. Top with chicken, onions and cilantro. Serve and enjoy.
